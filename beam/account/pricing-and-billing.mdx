title: "Pricing and Billing"
description:
  "Beam charges usage based billing for the compute used to train and deploy
  your apps."
---

<Warning>
  During our private beta, Beam is free to use. In the future, we will charge
  simple usage-based pricing.
</Warning>

### Monitoring compute usage

You can view all the compute you've consumed for the monthly billing period in
the **Plans and Billing** section of the **Settings** page.
