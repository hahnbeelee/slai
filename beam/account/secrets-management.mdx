<Warning>This feature is still under development. We will release it very shortly!</Warning>

### Adding a new secret

Secrets are managed in the **Settings** page, under **Secrets**. To create a new
secret, click **Create new secret.** You'll be prompted to enter a name and
value.

![](/img/beam/account/secrets-management.png)

### Using secrets inside your projects

You can access secrets inside your projects through the `os.environ()` object:

```
very_secret_key = os.environ['SLAI_VERY_SECRET_KEY']
```
